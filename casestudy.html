Success Story

How focused tuition support transformed a student's learning journey

This case study highlights the impact of personalised Maths and Science tuition for CBSE, ICSE, SSC and IGCSE students in Kalwa and nearby Thane areas.

Student challenge

A student was struggling to keep up with classwork and lacked confidence in mathematics and science. Concepts felt disconnected, and weekly tests were creating anxiety.

Our approach

  • • Small group teaching with more one-to-one interaction
  • • Step-by-step concept building instead of memorisation
  • • Regular revision and doubt-solving sessions
  • • Encouraging progress tracking and parent communication

Results

30%+

Improvement in test performance after focused support

Higher

Confidence and participation in class

Consistent

Homework completion and better study habits
