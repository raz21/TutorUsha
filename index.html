About Us

16 Years of Building Strong Academic Foundations

For over 16 years, Usha Tutorials has focused on providing the best quality of education — acquiring knowledge rather than merely imparting it, with teachers acting as facilitators. We make learning enjoyable through an unconventional methodology where students learn via research and model-making, leading to greater retention.

Our journey began with a simple yet profound goal - to bridge the gap between traditional classroom learning and true conceptual understanding.

Over the years, we've witnessed students struggling with vague comprehension, lack of confidence, and an inadequate support system. That's why we've dedicated ourselves to creating a comprehensive solution that combines personalized learning experiences, individualized approaches, and a proven methodology for academic excellence.

Our Teaching Philosophy is built on engaging students in more participatory ways with Math and Science subjects. Our classes are available to students of Std. III to Std. IX for CBSE, ICSE, IGCSE and SSC state board syllabus. We strive to help students comprehend subjects via living examples. We make certain that each student is individually assessed and taught properly. We have teaching techniques for putting the principles learned on paper into practice. We have a regular assignment structure in place to assist students in revising the ideas learned in class.

Our goal is to increase students' brainpower, logical thinking and decision-making abilities to set a strong academic foundation, ensuring the all-round development of every student's personality. We offer coaching for ICSE, CBSE, IB and SSC state board syllabi.
